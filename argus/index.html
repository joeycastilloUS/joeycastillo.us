Change nothing without your say.

Argus is a read-only operations console for the infrastructure you depend on. It watches the live state, checks it against best practice, and surfaces what needs you — then makes only the small, deliberate writes you explicitly allow, enforced on the server, never the browser.

Read-only by default

The console reads. Every write is a separate, allow-listed, server-enforced action — production stays physically un-writable unless you say otherwise.

Operational targets

Declare what "healthy" means — the devices, queues, SLAs you expect green. Argus shows green until reality diverges, then tells you exactly what slipped.

Audit + action plans

Continuously checks your config against best practice and hands you a fix for every finding — severity-ranked, no guessing.
