Purcius C99 Stack

← back to metal · every skill, every layer, every line owned by kASTIL — pure C99
One language law: C99 for everything we own; C# only for a thin jacket over a foreign manufacturer's API. The stack is four layers — bare-metal target runtime at the bottom, the AI substrate above it, the ADD engine that drives it, and the editions/gateways that ship it. 53 skills plus the biRT target layer. Nothing here is a framework; it is all metal.
Break-out — pure-C vs inference. Three classes: pure-C · deterministic inference infrastructure (2) inference criteria → should be .pi (4)  — the .pi tokens are criteria/prompts, not C. judge is not a pure-C skill; it should be .pi.
