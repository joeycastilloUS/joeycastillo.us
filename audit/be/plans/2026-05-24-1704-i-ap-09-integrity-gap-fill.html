§6 — What we GET from this (concrete capabilities)

  1. Full forensic trail — every triple store write/read/delete/share logged with (action, obj_id[32], actor_id[32], timestamp, prev_hash[32]). 39K ops/sec spec baseline.
  2. Tamper-proof history — walk entire chain from genesis to present. Break one entry, all downstream hashes fail. Detected instantly on audit_verify().
  3. Replay any historical state — hash-chain walk with visitor callback reconstructs store state at any prior timestamp. Forensic recovery + legal discovery.
  4. Actor accountability — every operation signed with actor_id[32]. Answers "who changed fact X on date Y?" with cryptographic proof.
  5. Immutable chain-of-custody — intel objects inherit audit history from capture (device + timestamp + hash at point 0). Court-admissible.
  6. Crash-safe — journal integration means audit entries survive power loss (unlike target-runtimes' volatile BSS ring buffer).
  7. Evidence chain maturity — "tamper-evident from birth" — enables investigative/legal workflows where provenance IS evidence.
