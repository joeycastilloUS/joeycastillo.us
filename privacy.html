Privacy Policy

Last updated June 21, 2026

This policy explains what the single sign-in and trip-planning apps operated by Joseph M. Castillo (joeycastillo.us, yosemite.camp, and connected sites — the “Apps”) collect, why, and what we never do. Short version: we collect the minimum to sign you in and connect you to your trip, we never sell it, and you can remove it.

What we collect when you log in with Discord

If you choose “Log in with Discord,” Discord shares only what its basic identify scope returns:

  • Your Discord user ID — to recognize you on return.
  • Your username and display name — to show who you are to your own group.
  • Your avatar image URL — used as your icon, if you have one.

We do not receive or store your Discord password, email, message history, or friend list. We never ask for a password — sign-in is handled entirely by Discord.

What else we store

  • A session cookie (yc_sess) so you stay signed in, and a short-lived state cookie during login. These are essential — no third-party advertising or tracking cookies are used.
  • What you add in the app — the trip you join, items you sign up for, your chosen icon, and notes you enter. This is yours and visible to your own group.

Why we use it

Only to run the app: identify you across the operator's sites, link you to the right person on a trip, show your sign-ups, and let your group coordinate. That's it.

Where it lives

Sign-in is brokered through joeycastillo.us and a private backend service; group data is stored in a private Google Firestore datastore over an encrypted connection. Some data is also cached on your own device so the app works offline, with no signal, on the trail.

What we never do

  • We never sell or rent your information.
  • We never show ads or run cross-site advertising trackers.
  • We never share your data with third parties except the services strictly needed to run sign-in (Discord) and storage (Google Cloud).

Your control

  • Log out anytime — it clears your session.
  • Remove yourself from a trip to take your participation out.
  • Delete your data — contact the operator via joeycastillo.us and we'll remove it.

Children

The Apps are personal tools for friends and family planning a trip and are not directed at children under 13. Kids in a family group are added by their own adults, who control that data.

Changes

If this policy changes, the “last updated” date above changes with it.

Contact

Questions or a deletion request? Reach the operator via joeycastillo.us.
